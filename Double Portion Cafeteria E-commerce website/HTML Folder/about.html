About Double Portion Cafeteria

Welcome to —your go-to spot for budget-friendly, delicious and filling meals, cozy ambiance, and great company. For over 15 years Double Portion Cafeteria has been a cornerstone of the Redeemer's university community on the campus, we've been serving fresh, flavorful, and heartwarming meals. Founded by Mr. Olusoji and the late Mrs. Folashade Oladepo, our journey began as a small kitchen service in Lagos and has since grown into the most trusted dining spot on campus.

Our commitment goes beyond just food- we aim to create a welcoming atmosphere where students, staff, and visitors can enjoy meals made with love and care. Whether you're here for a quick breakfast, a hearty lunch, or a relaxing dinner, we promise a great dining experience.
